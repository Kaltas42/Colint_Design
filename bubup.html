KRAFT-HEINZ: BUBUP

Baby food branding, packaging design for the product and a promotional poster

Brief

Design a brand for Kraft-Heinz’s Bubup baby food and create a packaging design for Spicers merchant group to use to promote different paper stocks that can be used for packaging.

Concept

Vibrant bubbly brand for parents to understand that it is a baby food brand. Adding bubble elements and a groovy patterns to attract attention. The information on the box explains the selling points to engage parents to purchase BUBUP BRAND the product.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Dimension.

About this project

The goal of this packaging design is to increase the rate of leads, specifications, enquiries and orders for packaging products for Spicers. The call to action is to contact a Spicers & Direct Paper representative for a sample, mockup, more information and the websites.
