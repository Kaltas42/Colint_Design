CITRUS TANG

FMCG Promotional Campaign Icypole packaging design

Brief

Design an artwork for the promotion of a new product ‘Citrus Tang’. Involves designing a package, and then mocking it up in 3D.

Concept

A spiral splash of the flavour orange and lime with the icypoles centered to highlight the product. The Citrus Tang banner with the orange and lime to show what flavours this product has. The bright orange sunburst background is used to engage the audience to make it seem fun and interesting.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Dimension.
